Privacy Policy

ThawUp — Guided Stretching
Effective: May 13, 2026 · Last updated: May 14, 2026

The short version: ThawUp keeps your data on your device. We don't sell your information, we don't use ad or analytics SDKs, and we don't have servers that store your stretching history. Subscriptions are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. That's it.

This Privacy Policy explains how Colin Robey, operating as CR Studios ("CR Studios," "we," "us," or "our"), handles information when you use the ThawUp mobile application (the "App"). By using ThawUp you agree to the practices described here.

If you have questions, contact us at crstudiosdev@gmail.com.

1. Summary

  • ThawUp is designed to minimize data collection.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.
  • Your stretching activity, routines, and history are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscriptions are handled by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlement state.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information stored locally on your device

The following information is created and stored on your device only. We do not have access to it.

  • Routines you choose or favorite
  • Session history (which stretches you completed, duration, date/time)
  • Streak and progress data
  • App preferences (sound, haptics, default durations)
  • Your acknowledgment of the in-app safety disclaimer

This data lives in the App's local database (SwiftData) and is removed when you delete the App.

b. Subscription information (via Apple and RevenueCat)

If you purchase a Premium subscription:

  • Payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment method, billing address, or full Apple ID.
  • We use RevenueCat (a subscription management platform) to verify your subscription status and provide receipt validation. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous app-generated user ID, your country code, device type, app version, and Apple's subscription receipt. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • We use this information only to grant or restore your Premium access — never for marketing or profiling.

c. Diagnostic and crash data (Apple only, optional)

If you have enabled "Share with App Developers" in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements, Apple may share anonymized crash logs and diagnostic data with us. This is controlled entirely by your iOS settings and contains no personally identifying information.

d. Information we do NOT collect

  • Name, email, phone number (unless you email us directly)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Health, medical, or biometric data of any kind
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing or web history

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide the App's core features (routines, history, charts)
  • To verify and manage your subscription (via Apple and RevenueCat)
  • To respond to you if you contact support

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside operating the App.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We share information only with:

  • Apple, when you purchase a subscription or request a refund through the App Store.
  • RevenueCat, solely to manage subscription entitlements (see Section 2b).
  • Law enforcement or courts, only when legally compelled, and only the minimum required.

5. Data Security

We rely on Apple's platform security and sandboxed app storage protections, and ThawUp's app data is stored with the iOS "complete until first user authentication" file protection class. Because data never leaves your device for our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your ThawUp data is limited to your own device's security.

No system is perfectly secure. You are responsible for safeguarding access to your device and Apple ID.

6. Children's Privacy

ThawUp is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK), and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Subscription: manage or cancel at iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions or at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.
  • Delete your data: delete the App from your device. Local data is removed.
  • Access, correction, deletion requests (GDPR / CCPA / Quebec Law 25 / PIPEDA): email crstudiosdev@gmail.com with your request. Because we hold minimal personal data, most requests are satisfied by deleting the App.
  • California residents: you have the right under the CCPA/CPRA to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information.

8. International Users

We are based in Ontario, Canada. If you use the App from outside Canada, you understand that your information (limited as described above) may be processed in Canada and the United States, which may have data protection laws different from those in your country.
